How it works

  • Tap main timer or ▶️/⏸️ to Start / Pause / Resume.
  • Forward (paused) ⏩ jumps to the next non-zero timer. Wrap increases round.
  • Back (paused) ⏪ first press restarts current; press again goes to previous non-zero.
  • YouTube: choose which timers play. Playlist Bank lets you save 15 links with labels.
  • Lock: double-double-tap anywhere to lock; double-tap to unlock (or double-double if enabled).
